= \graphic(tool-polygon.png) Polygon =
label:tool_polygon
The Polygon tool draws polygon shapes.

*Settings*
||\image(linewidth.png)|| Line Width || The width of the polygon edge ||
||\graphic(linestyle-solid.png) \graphic(linestyle-long-dash.png) || Line Style || Solid or dashed ||
||\graphic(fillstyle-border.png) \graphic(fillstyle-fill.png) \graphic(fillstyle-border-and-fill.png)|| Fill Style || Outline only, Fill only or Fill and Outline ||

*Keyboard modifiers*
||\image(key-shift.png)||The first line connected to the moved point will be constrained to 45 degree angles.||
||\image(key-ctrl.png)\image(key-shift.png)||The second line connected to the moved point will be constrained to 45 degree angles.||
||\image(key-ctrl.png)||The moved point will snap to \ref(feat-snap-points.txt,points in objects), the \ref(feat-grid.txt,grid) or \ref(feat-snap90.txt,90 degree corner points).||
